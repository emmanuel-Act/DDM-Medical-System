Mission

The mission of DDM medical system is to provide relevant and quality
medical system that will tackle the major problem in the medical sector
which is delayed diagnosis. This may cause some serious problems which
may even some times cause death. So, our main target is to enable
people to have an easy access to medical treatemnt at home and provide
a fast and reliable appointment that saves our customers a lot of time

Vision

DDM aims to better the outdated medical system in Ethiopia and
significantly decrease the number of deathes caused by delayed diagnosis.

Values

The values of DDM medical system are Intellectual honesty, sympathy, hardworking,
humanity, and love.
